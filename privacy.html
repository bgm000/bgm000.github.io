Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

KVE Video Scheduler respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data:

  • Data Collection: We only collect TikTok OAuth tokens necessary for video publishing.
  • Data Storage: Tokens are stored securely and are not shared with third parties.
  • Data Usage: Tokens are used solely to publish videos on your behalf via the TikTok Content Posting API.
  • Data Deletion: You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
